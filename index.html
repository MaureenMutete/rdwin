Welcome to Our Space

Regional Deaf Women Initiative Network is a dedicated organization committed to the empowerment of Deaf women and girls in Kenya. RDWIN was founded in 2003 by 3 deaf ladies to address the challenges facing deaf women and girls. Registered in 2007, the network has evolved to be a feminist centre of excellence that strives to create a supportive and inclusive community where Deaf women and girls can thrive, connect, and inspire one another

Learn More

Mission

Our mission is to empower Deaf women and girls by promoting equality, fostering personal and professional growth, and advocating for their rights within society. We aim to create a platform where they can share experiences, access resources, and develop the skills needed to lead fulfilling lives.

Vision

We envision a world where Deaf women and girls are fully included, respected, and able to participate equally in all aspects of life. We aspire to break down barriers, challenge stereotypes, and create opportunities that enable Deaf women to achieve their full potential.

Empowerment

We believe in the strength and potential of Deaf women and girls. Our programs and initiatives are designed to equip them with the tools and confidence needed to succeed.

Inclusivity

We are committed to creating an environment where all Deaf women, regardless of background, can feel welcomed and valued.

Advocacy

We actively advocate for the rights and recognition of Deaf women, working to ensure their voices are heard at every level of society.

Community

We foster a sense of community among Deaf women and girls, providing a space for support, connection, and shared experiences.

Equality

We champion equality and work towards eliminating the systemic barriers that prevent Deaf women from accessing the opportunities

Our Services

Educational Workshops

Tailored workshops using accessible formats focused on leadership, personal development, and vocational skills.

Mentorship Programs

Connecting young Deaf girls with experienced Deaf women mentors to guide and inspire them for growth.

Community Outreach

We offer initiatives aimed at raising awareness and building support networks within the broader community.

Resource Center

We are a comprehensive hub offering resources, guides, information on the available services and support to our community.

Impact

Since our inception, we have positively impacted the lives of countless Deaf women and girls by providing them with the skills, knowledge, and support they need to succeed. Our programs have helped participants build confidence, develop leadership skills, and connect with a wider community of Deaf women.

Join Us

Whether you are looking to volunteer, become a member, or support our work through donations, there are many ways to get involved with the Regional Deaf Women Initiative Network. Together, we can create a brighter future for Deaf women and girls in Kenya.
